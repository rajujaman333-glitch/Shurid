Our History

A Journey of 27 Years of Unity and Excellence

Today, ‘Suhrid 18 Samobai Samity Ltd.’ stands as one of the most vibrant, prestigious, and deeply-rooted fraternal organizations within the 18th BCS Administration Service of Bangladesh. Founded by the officers of 18th BCS Administrations Cadres. The organization has successfully transitioned from a closed batch platform into a highly organized, diversified, and impactful welfare collective. Over a glorious journey spanning 27 years, Suhrid 18 has consistently combined organizational excellence with social responsibility, setting a benchmark for batch based solidarity in the civil service.

The Early Days & Evolution

Since its early days, the core mission of Suhrid 18 has been to foster deeply rooted bonding, creativity, and mutual cooperation among its members. Over nearly three decades, the platform has expanded its horizons significantly. What began as a traditional network of administrative colleagues has evolved into a fully operational co-operative system that manages multi-dimensional initiatives, including structured investment activities, welfare programs, and family-centric engagements.

Today, the name Suhrid 18 is synonymous with trust, empathy, and an unbreakable emotional bond—often referred to by its members not just as an organization, but as a lifestyle and a shared identity.

A Legacy of Milestones and Core Pillars

Throughout its 27 year history, Suhrid 18 has crossed numerous milestones, driven by the dedication of its members and executive leadership. The organization’s operations are anchored around four distinct pillars:

  • Sustained Investment & Member Welfare: Embracing a cooperative model, the organization manages strategic investment initiatives that ensure financial security and mutual benefits for its members.
  • Institutional Memory & Creativity: A cornerstone of the organization’s cultural identity is its flagship regular publication, ‘Angina’. For years, this commemorative magazine has served as a creative mirror, capturing the administrative excellence, literary talents, and shared memories of the Suhrid family.
  • Compassionate Care & Solidarity: True to its name the organization has a long standing history of standing firmly by its members and their families during times of illness, adversity, or crisis.
  • Social Responsibility & Intellectual Growth: Beyond internal welfare, Suhrid 18 actively engages in community development and humanitarian efforts. The platform also hosts joint 'Learning Sessions' on contemporary issues to ensure continuous professional and intellectual enrichment.

Bridging Generations for the Future

As Suhrid 18 continues to grow, its impact has naturally extended to the families and loved ones of the members. A unique and defining achievement of recent years has been the active integration of the next generation ‘Projonmo Suhrid’ into this joyful journey. By involving children and family members in annual get-togethers, cultural exchanges, and collaborative events, Suhrid 18 is successfully transferring its core values of integrity, unity, and empathy from one generation to the next.

Looking forward, Suhrid 18 is uniquely positioned to capitalize on its rich history and collective wisdom. Managed by dedicated executive teams, the organization aims to further scale its welfare operations, diversify its social impact initiatives, and ensure that this beautiful bond of love and excellence remains illuminated for decades to come.
